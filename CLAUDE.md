# Understory Collaborative — Claude Guidelines

## Content & Communication

- **Never assume — always ask.** When writing content (case studies, marketing copy, documentation, or any client-facing material), do not fill in gaps with assumptions. If information is missing, unclear, or ambiguous, ask the user before proceeding. Leave placeholders or flag open questions rather than inventing details.
- This applies to: tone, framing, technical details, timelines, metrics, quotes, role descriptions, and editorial characterizations.
- When in doubt, surface the question. It's always better to ask than to guess wrong.
