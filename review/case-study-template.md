### The Challenge

_2–3 paragraphs. Describe the client's situation before engaging Understory._

**Guiding questions:**
- What business problem or opportunity brought them to us?
- What had they tried before? Why didn't it work?
- What constraints were they operating under (budget, timeline, compliance, team capacity)?
- Which persona does this client most closely resemble? (Reference `personas.md`)

---

### Our Approach

_2–3 paragraphs. Describe how Understory engaged with the client._

**Map to the Root-Rise-Flourish framework where applicable:**

**Root (Discovery & Strategy)**
- What did we learn during discovery?
- What strategic decisions did we help the client make?
- How did we challenge or validate their assumptions?

**Rise (Build & Iterate)**
- What was the delivery methodology? (Agile sprints, phased rollout, etc.)
- How did we collaborate with the client's internal team?
- What pivots or adjustments were made along the way?

**Flourish (Deliver & Sustain)**
- How was the solution delivered and transitioned?
- What knowledge transfer or documentation was provided?
- What ongoing support or relationship exists?

## Writing Guidelines

**Voice & Tone:**
- Write in Understory's collaborative voice — "we" not "our team of experts"
- Be specific and honest — don't inflate results
- Lead with the client's story, not Understory's capabilities
- Avoid jargon when business language will do

**For the Marketing Review Group:**
- Does this case study speak to at least one persona's goals and objections?
- Are the results concrete and credible?
- Does the narrative reinforce Understory's positioning (human-centric, experienced, holistic)?

**For the Client Critique Group:**
- Reading as your assigned persona, does this case study build confidence?
- What questions does it leave unanswered?
- Would you forward this to a colleague or decision-maker?

**Photography / Visuals (when available):**
- Product screenshots (with client permission)
- Before/after comparisons
- Architecture diagrams (simplified for non-technical readers)
- Data visualizations of results
