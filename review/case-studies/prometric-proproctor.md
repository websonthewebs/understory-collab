### The Challenge

Prometric is one of the world's largest testing and assessment providers, administering high-stakes professional and academic exams at centers around the globe. When in-person testing halted in early 2020, demand for remote proctoring surged overnight.

Prometric's existing remote assessment platform, ProProctor, wasn't built to handle the volume. Students facing final exams and professionals pursuing certifications needed to test from home — and the platform had to deliver the same security, reliability, and human support they'd expect at a physical testing center.

The stakes were high in every sense: exam integrity, candidate experience, and Prometric's reputation as an industry leader all depended on getting this right, fast.

### Key Takeaways

- **Crisis creates urgency — and opportunity.** The pandemic forced a complete rethink of remote proctoring, and the tight timeline demanded focused execution with no room for scope creep.
- **Platform choices matter.** Building on Twilio Flex and Programmable Video gave Prometric a customizable, scalable foundation rather than a rigid off-the-shelf solution.
- **Video is infrastructure, not a feature.** In high-stakes remote testing, video reliability directly affects exam integrity and candidate trust — it's not a nice-to-have.
